<h1 class="adar-archive-title">Video art by Guillermo Ordás</h1>
<p class="adar-archive-lede">
Video art piece by the Asturian visual artist Guillermo Ordás, presented as part of ADAR Festival 2023. An audiovisual intervention in dialogue with the rural heritage and sites where the festival's programme takes place.
</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="adar-archive-body">
<p>
Guillermo Ordás created a video art piece specifically for ADAR Festival, weaving moving image, rhythm and visual matter into the atmosphere of the heritage sites the festival activates. The work explores the relationship between territory, memory and contemporary gaze through the artist's distinctive sensibility.
</p>

<p>
The inclusion of video art and audiovisual works in the festival's programme reflects ADAR's interdisciplinary vocation: crossing music, performance and visual arts in a single curatorial gesture, expanding the languages with which the rural can be inhabited.
</p>
