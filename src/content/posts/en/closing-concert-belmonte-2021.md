<h1 class="adar-archive-title">Closing Concert in Belmonte de Miranda (2021)</h1>
<p class="adar-archive-lede">
The closing of the inaugural edition of Festival ADAR in Belmonte de Miranda, capital of the municipality where the project is based, in August 2021.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="adar-archive-body">
<p>
The inaugural edition of Festival ADAR came to a close in Belmonte de Miranda, capital of the municipality that includes Leiguarda, the home of the project. This concert marked the birth of the festival and laid the foundations of its model: chamber music in villages, churches and heritage spaces across rural Asturias.
</p>
<p>
From that first edition came the commitment to return every August, expanding edition after edition the map of municipalities, spaces and artists that pass through ADAR.
</p>
