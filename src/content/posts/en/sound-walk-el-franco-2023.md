<h1 class="adar-archive-title">Sound walk (El Franco)</h1>

<p class="adar-archive-lede">
Before the concert at the As Quintas Auditorium, the town of El Franco gathered at the Town Hall Square and María Cristina Park, where musicians performed Bach pieces at different corners.
</p>

<div class="adar-archive-body">
<p>
Before presenting the full concert at the As Quintas Auditorium, the town gathered at the Town Hall Square and María Cristina Park, where musicians performed various Bach pieces at different corners. Attendees followed them, and the entire walk concluded with a performance of Mozart's Flute Quartet K285.
</p>
<p>
This sound walk was accompanied by the warmth and brief interviews conducted by Asturian host Laude Martínez. He spoke not only with the musicians and Festival directors but also with local artists and El Franco's culture councillor.
</p>
