<h1 class="adar-archive-title">Artistic Installation outside the Palacio de Fontela (Grado, 2024)</h1>
<p class="adar-archive-lede">
An artistic installation and a nocturnal sound walk outside the Palacio de Fontela, part of the 17 August programme of Festival ADAR 2024.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="adar-archive-body">
<p>
Following the concert at the Palacio de Miranda-Valdecarzana and the micro-concert at the Capilla de los Dolores, the night closed with an intervention outside the Palacio de Fontela: an artistic installation combined with a sound walk around the building.
</p>
<p>
The action explored the relationship between historic architecture, nocturnal landscape and itinerant sonic experience, one of the recurring threads in the festival's programming in Grado.
</p>
