<h1 class="adar-archive-title">Concert at the Monastery of San Antolín de Bedón, Llanes</h1>
<p class="adar-archive-lede">
The 13th-century Romanesque monastery was transformed into an immersive performance space where music, architecture and light engaged with the audience in a circular format.
</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="adar-archive-video">

</div>

<div class="adar-archive-body">
<p>
The audience surrounded the musicians inside the monastery, creating an intimate and enveloping experience. The lighting, designed specifically for the space, engaged with the ancient stone and turned the concert into a complete sensory experience.
</p>
</div>
