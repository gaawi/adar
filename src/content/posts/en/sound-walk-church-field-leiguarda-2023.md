<h1 class="adar-archive-title">Sound Walk with Video Mapping at the Campo de la Iglesia (Leiguarda, 2023)</h1>
<p class="adar-archive-lede">
A nocturnal sound walk through the Campo de la Iglesia of Leiguarda with video mapping projections on the church façade. The closing of the 12 August programme.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="adar-archive-body">
<p>
The church of Leiguarda became a screen and a resonating chamber in an intervention that combined sound walk and audiovisual projections. The musicians spread out across the church field while the audience moved through the space beneath the images projected onto the stone.
</p>
<p>
It was the closing of an intense day that had included a sound walk at the Museo de las Ayalgas and a micro-concert at the Panera Casantos, forming a coherent sequence between ethnographic heritage, rural architecture and religious architecture.
</p>
