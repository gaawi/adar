<h1 class="adar-archive-title">Cider and Empanada Gathering (Leiguarda, 2024)</h1>
<p class="adar-archive-lede">
Cider and empanada for everyone in Leiguarda as the communal closing of the 2024 edition of Festival ADAR.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="adar-archive-body">
<p>
After the closing concert around the Church of Leiguarda, the night carried on with an open gathering for the whole audience and the artists: cider, empanada and conversation. A communal closing that works as an intrinsic part of the festival, a bond between the village and those who visit it during these days of programming.
</p>
<p>
The espicha is one of the most characteristic social formats of Asturian tradition, and ADAR embraces it as a cultural action in its own right.
</p>
