<h1 class="adar-archive-title">Closing Concert in Belmonte de Miranda (2022)</h1>
<p class="adar-archive-lede">
As the closing of the ADAR Festival's second edition, the musicians presented their final concert in the capital of the municipality where the association is based: Belmonte de Miranda. On a circular stage in the town park, chamber music merged with the sound of the river and the surrounding nature.
</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="adar-archive-body">
<p>
The final concert of the 2022 edition took place in the Park of Belmonte de Miranda, a space that offered a circular stage surrounded by vegetation and bathed in the murmur of the river. It was a symbolic closing: the musicians performed in the heart of the municipality that hosts ADAR, returning to the local community the music that had travelled across Asturias throughout the week.
</p>

<p>
The music of Brahms, Dvořák, Shaw, Mozart, and Jofre blended with the flow of the river, creating an experience where classical music and nature met in perfect balance. The setting invited contemplative listening, and the audience, seated around the stage, experienced an intimate and warm closing.
</p>

<h3 style="margin-top:28px">Programme</h3>

<p>
<strong>Johannes Brahms</strong> – Quintet for Clarinet and Strings in B minor, Op. 115<br>
<strong>Antonín Dvořák</strong> – American Quartet in F major, Op. 96<br>
<strong>Caroline Shaw</strong> – Blueprint<br>
<strong>Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart</strong> – Quintet for Clarinet and Strings in A major, KV 581<br>
<strong>JP Jofre</strong> – Como el Agua and Primavera
</p>
