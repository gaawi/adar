<h1 class="adar-archive-title">Micro-concert at the Panera Casantos (Leiguarda, 2023)</h1>
<p class="adar-archive-lede">
An intimate micro-concert at the Panera Casantos, part of the Leiguarda 2023 strand, with limited capacity. Chamber music in one of the most characteristic spaces of traditional Asturian architecture.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="adar-archive-body">
<p>
The Panera Casantos, one of the emblematic hórreos of Leiguarda, hosted a limited-capacity micro-concert during the night of 12 August 2023. A day that combined a sound walk at the Museo de las Ayalgas, a micro-concert in the panera itself, and a nocturnal sound walk with video mapping at the Campo de la Iglesia.
</p>
<p>
The brief format made it possible to experiment with the particular acoustics of the wooden structure raised on pegollos, a hallmark of rural Asturian architecture.
</p>
