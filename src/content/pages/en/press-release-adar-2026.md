ADAR Festival · <em>Edition VI</em> · 2026.

Eleven concerts from 4 to 16 August 2026 across seven heritage venues in rural Asturias. Chamber music, site-specific interventions and a closing quintet with espicha at the Church of Leiguarda. All programming is free entry with RSVP on Eventbrite.

<p class="qea-eyebrow">§ 01 · Headline</p>
<h2 class="qea-h">
ADAR Festival returns to rural Asturias for its <em>sixth edition</em>.
</h2>
<p class="qea-lede">
The <strong>Association for the Development of the Arts in Rural Areas</strong> presents the VI edition of ADAR Festival: twelve consecutive nights of contemporary classical music, two site-specific interventions in Leiguarda and a residency showcase. The programme spans seven heritage venues across Belmonte de Miranda, Cangas del Narcea, El Franco, Villanueva de Oscos, Llanes, and two hórreos selected by the 2026 Hórreos & Paneras Competition — whose venues are announced on 25 July.
</p>
