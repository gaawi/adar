<div>
<p class="adar-eyebrow">What is ADAR</p>
<h2>Revitalizing rural areas<br>through the living arts</h2>
<p class="adar-lede">
ADAR transforms villages and small towns into stages for contemporary art, creating bridges between tradition, innovation, and community.
We design demanding artistic experiences with careful production, mediation, and audiovisual documentation.
</p>
</div>

<div class="adar-card">
<p class="adar-card-title">Association for the Development of the Arts in Rural Areas</p>
<p>
Founded in 2020 in Leiguarda by Guillermo Laporta and Josefina Urraca.
We promote residencies, site-specific concerts, installations, mediation, and audiovisual projects.
</p>
<p>
Our approach combines a strong local dimension (heritage, landscape, community) with national and European ambition for networks, partnerships, and future calls.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</section>

<section class="adar-section adar-cream" id="mision">
<div class="adar-wrap">
<div class="adar-head">
<p class="adar-eyebrow">Our mission</p>
<h2>Cultural, social, and territorial impact</h2>
<p class="adar-sub">
ADAR is a cultural revitalization tool with contemporary standards, designed to engage with the Asturian, national, and European cultural ecosystem.
</p>
</div>

<div class="adar-features">
<div class="adar-feature">
<h3>Cultural revitalization</h3>
<p>International-impact projects in rural areas.</p>
</div>

<div class="adar-feature">
<h3>Access and mediation</h3>
<p>Close, meaningful experiences for local and visiting audiences.</p>
</div>

<div class="adar-feature">
<h3>Activated heritage</h3>
<p>Churches, chapels, hórreos, rural architecture, and outdoor installations.</p>
</div>

<div class="adar-feature">
<h3>Networks and circulation</h3>
<p>Bridges between Asturias, Spain, and Europe for artists and presenters.</p>
</div>

<div class="adar-feature">
<h3>Contemporary creation</h3>
<p>Residencies, new works, hybrid formats, and audiovisual production.</p>
</div>

<div class="adar-feature">
<h3>Art with purpose</h3>
<p>Projects that engage with current challenges through artistic practice.</p>
</div>
