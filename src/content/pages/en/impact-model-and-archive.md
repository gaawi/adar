<div>
<p class="ai-eyebrow">2. The ADAR model</p>
<h2>A hybrid structure between creation and territory</h2>
<p class="ai-lede">Site-specific programming, residencies, mediation, and archive within the same ecosystem, with local return and external circulation.</p>
<ul class="ai-list">
<li>Site-specific programming with careful production</li>
<li>Residencies that generate new works and processes</li>
<li>Mediation with local and visiting audiences</li>
<li>Audiovisual documentation as memory and outreach</li>
<li>Regional, national, and international networks and partnerships</li>
</ul>
</div>
<aside class="ai-panel">
<p class="ai-panel-title">How it translates into practice</p>
Space selection, dialogue with the location, technical needs, audience experience, narrative and documentation.
<p class="ai-muted">The result exists as a live experience and as a reusable archive.</p>
