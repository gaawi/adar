<p class="ed-eyebrow">§ 01 · Allies</p>
<h2 class="ed-h">National and <em>international</em> collaborations.</h2>
<p class="ed-lede">
Active alliances and ongoing conversations with organisations, festivals and networks that extend the project's reach. A porous network between Asturias and the rest of the world.
</p>
</section>

<section class="ed-objectives">
<p class="ed-eyebrow">§ 02 · Active network</p>
<h2 class="ed-h">Who walks with ADAR.</h2>
<ol class="ed-list">
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">01</span><div><strong>CreArtBox.</strong> New York · chamber music and multimedia art collective led by Guillermo Laporta and Josefina Urraca. <a href="https://creartbox.nyc/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">creartbox.nyc</a></div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">02</span><div><strong>Porto PianoFest.</strong> Porto, Portugal · international festival focused on contemporary piano and repertoire, with curatorial affinity. <a href="https://www.portopianofest.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">portopianofest.com</a></div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">03</span><div><strong>Opus 71 Concerts.</strong> New York · chamber music concert series and collaboration platform. <a href="https://www.opus71concerts.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">opus71concerts.com</a></div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">04</span><div><strong>Paris Chamber Music Circle.</strong> Paris, France · chamber music network with international reach. Direction: Kyle Collins.</div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">05</span><div><strong>Asociación Musical Diego Silva Montero.</strong> Malpartida, Cáceres · promoter of contemporary music with territorial focus.</div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">06</span><div><strong>Ballarte Festival.</strong> Benasque, Huesca · multidisciplinary festival aligned with ADAR's philosophy of territorial activation. <a href="https://ballartefestival.benasque.com/es" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ballartefestival.benasque.com</a></div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">07</span><div><strong>Paradores.</strong> Spain · hotel network in historic buildings and natural settings, with ties to residency and heritage.</div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">08</span><div><strong>Escuela Superior de Música Reina Sofía.</strong> Madrid · collaboration through the GreenME programme in sound walks.</div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">09</span><div><strong>Caja Rural de Asturias.</strong> Main patron through the Compromiso CR programme.</div></li>
</ol>
</section>

<p class="ed-eyebrow">§ 03 · Join the network</p>
<h2 class="ed-h">Want to collaborate <em>with ADAR</em>?</h2>
<div class="ed-twocol">
<div>
<p>
If you are an organisation, festival, institution or company, we will be glad to explore avenues of collaboration, co-production or in-kind support. Every alliance is built in conversation from the start: what your organisation brings and what ADAR can activate in return.
</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>
To propose a collaboration, write to <a href="mailto:leiguardamusic@gmail.com">leiguardamusic@gmail.com</a> with a short introduction, the kind of collaboration you imagine and your timing. We reply to every proposal.
</p>
