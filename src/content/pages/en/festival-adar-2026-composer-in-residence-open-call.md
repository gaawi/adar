<p class="ed-eyebrow">§ 01 · Open call</p>
<h2 class="ed-h">Composer-in-<em>Residence</em> 2026.</h2>
<p class="ed-lede">
The ADAR composition residency for the 2026 edition offers a flexible format — in-person, hybrid or virtual — and culminates with a featured performance during the festival in August, at a heritage venue in rural Asturias.
</p>
</section>

<section class="ed-twocol">
<div>
<p class="ed-kicker mono">Base and setting</p>
<h3>A village as studio.</h3>
<p>
The in-person residency takes place in Leiguarda (Belmonte de Miranda), a hundred-soul village with a fitted rehearsal hall, grand piano and lodging.
</p>
</div>
<div>
<p class="ed-kicker mono">Premiere</p>
<h3>Work performed at the festival.</h3>
<p>
The commissioned work premieres during Festival ADAR 2026, with festival musicians and professional audiovisual recording for further circulation.
</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="ed-objectives">
<p class="ed-eyebrow">§ 02 · Official terms</p>
<h2 class="ed-h">What the residency offers.</h2>
<ol class="ed-list">
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">01</span><div><strong>Commission of new work.</strong> For chamber ensemble (2 to 6 performers) or solo piece with electronics.</div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">02</span><div><strong>Premiere at the festival.</strong> Performance in August 2026 at a selected heritage venue.</div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">03</span><div><strong>Fee and lodging.</strong> Commission fee, lodging and per diem during the in-person stay.</div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">04</span><div><strong>Audiovisual documentation.</strong> Professional recording for circulation, press and festival archive.</div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">05</span><div><strong>Mentorship.</strong> Support from the artistic co-direction and the performers throughout the process.</div></li>
<li><span class="ed-list-n mono">06</span><div><strong>Flexible format.</strong> In-person, hybrid or virtual: the model adapts to the selected composer's needs.</div></li>
</ol>
</section>
